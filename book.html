Basic Electrical And Electronics Engineering

The course introduces to the concept of electrical and electronics components, devices and circuits and how they function in the real world.

Communication Skills

This course focus on Communication Skills as being able to communicate efficiently and effectively is perhaps the most significant of all the life skills.

Computational Mathematics

This course is to provide students with the mathematical concepts necessary for computing and to impart analytical ability.

Problem Solving With Programming

The course fully covers the basics of programming in the “C” programming language and demonstrates fundamental programming techniques, customs and vocabulary.

This course offers a basic understanding of different AI techniques in various applications and how you can build upon it using already available datasets/ models.

The course begins with the study of crystal structure of solids and then extended to semiconductors. The students will learn to apply the principles of various semiconducting components.
